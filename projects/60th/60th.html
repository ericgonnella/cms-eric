60th Anniversary Webpage

Ocean County College

Desktop


As the College was entering its 60th year of operation it was decided that a 60th anniversary page was needed.

The result was a page that provided a fun interactive overview for the visitors while also giving a sense of the college's history.

Some highlighted features include: Then-Now Slider, Image Gallery, Interactive timeline, and form input.

Mobile


For the mobile site we ensured that the main slider functionality was carried over from the desktop to provide micro-interactions for users to engage with the webpage.

The idea for utilizing a slideshow was considered for the images, but we wanted users to scroll and see the full resolution to increase the impact of the page.

Utilizing section separators provided a new and fresh appearance for each of the users to ensure they would know that content is still available as they continued to scroll.
