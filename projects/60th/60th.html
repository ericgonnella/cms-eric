60th Anniversary Webpage

Ocean County College

◄ Back to Projects

Desktop


As the College was entering its 60th year of operation it was decided that a 60th anniversary page was needed.

The result was a page that provided a fun interaractive overview for the visitors while also giving a sense of the college's history.

Some highlighted features include: Then-Now Slider, Image Gallery, Interactive timeline, and form input.

Mobile


For the mobile site we ensure that the main slider functionality was carried over from the desktop to proivde micro-interactions for users to engage in the webpage.

The idea for utilizing slideshow was considered for the images, but we wanted users to scroll and see the full resolution to increase the impact of the page.

Utilizing section seperators provided a new and fresh appearance for each of the users to ensure they would know that content is still avaialbe as the contiuned to scroll.
